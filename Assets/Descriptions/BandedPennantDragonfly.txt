Banded Pennant Dragonfly, "Celithemis Faschiata"
  
Physical description: Long and slender with four wings, having black dots as patterns on said wings. Bodies are black with gold spots on the back.
  
Ecological Function: Larvae are predators that lie in wait to ambush their prey, while adults will hawk-dive other insects from above. Eats mosquitoes while being eaten by frogs, birds, and fish.
  
Lives up to five years underwater before becoming an adult.
  
Lives statewide near water.
